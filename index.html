About

In 2003, Mr. Shashikant Kedar launched Creative Computers in Sangola, providing services to schools, colleges, banks and local businesses. Today, Creative Computers has evolved into a distinguished brand, celebrated for its exceptional service and cutting-edge solutions, including broadband, extending its reach across Sangola,Mangalwedha, Pandharpur, Jath and Sangli. Celebrating 20 years of excellence, Creative Computers continues to earn the trust and loyalty of its customers.
